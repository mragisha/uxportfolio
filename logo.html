Behind The Logo



My logo is a Butterfly which has my initials NJ warped into it for a personalized touch.

I was always and still am in awe of the metamorphosis that a butterfly goes through and its transformation into a beautiful creature from a caterpillar.
While I relate to all the phases of the butterflies' transformation, the reason/rationale behind me choosing this is the fact that it flies without any restrictions and is so difficult to be withheld at a spot, a trait that I find is predominant in me. I let my thoughts fly around just like a butterfly without any restriction.
The other thing that inspires me is the role of butterflies in the pollination process. The fact that they can unite immobile flowers and help create something as beautiful as life is fascinating and something I relate to. I am very keen on social responsibility and like to help others develop into better individuals with the knowledge I have.

The logo has a few rounded and some sharp edges defining the balanced nature of my life, where I am sometimes fun loving, jocular and at other times sincere when it comes to my career or when I need to stand by expectations.
